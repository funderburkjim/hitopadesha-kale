<s>cAndrAyaRavratam</s>--an expiatory penance regulated by the moon's age. During its observance the daily quantity of food which consists of fifteen mouthfuls on the full-moon day is to be diminished by one mouthful every day during the dark fortnight till it is reduced to zero on the new-moon-day, and then to be increased in like manner during the bright fortnight. The Samans ought to be chanted all the while by the observer of this vow. Vide the Smrti of Vasishtha quoted in the com.; also Manu. XI, 216 and Yajnavalkya. II, 324.
