Usha has a Hindi version of hitopadesha.
; Commentary- Abhinava-Raja-Lakshmi
; Author- Guruprasada Shastri
; With Hindi Translation

In part 4 (sanDiH), she noticed a difference between Kale's version and
the Hindi version for Shlokas 22-25
; ---------------------------------
; Kale
yo hi DarmaM puraskftya hitvA BartuH priyApriye .
apriyARyAha taTyAni tena rAjA sahAyavAn .. 22..

yudDe vinASo Bavati kadAciduBayorapi .
nahi saMSayitaM kuryAdityuvAca bfhaspatiH .. 23..

suhfdbalaM taTA rAjyamAtmAnaM kIrtimeva ca .
yuDi saMdehadolAsTaM ko hi kuryAdabAliSaH .. 24..

saMDimicCetsamenApi saMdigDo vijayo yuDi .
sundopasundAvanyonyaM nazwO tulyabalO na kim .. 25 ..

; -----------------------------------------
; Hindi version
yo hi DarmaM puraskftya hitvA BartuH priyApriye .
apriyARyAha paTyAni tena rAjA sahAyavAn .. 22..
 note: paTyAni instead of taTyAni
   paTya suitable;  taTya truth
   
suhfdbalaM taTA rAjyamAtmAnaM kIrtimeva ca .
yuDi saMdehadolAsTaM ko hi kuryAdabAliSaH .. 23..

saMDimicCetsamenApi saMdigDo vijayo yuDi .
nahi saMSayitaM kuryAdityuvAca bfhaspatiH .. 24..

yudDe vinASo Bavati kadAciduBayorapi .
sundopasundAvanyonyaM nazwO tulyabalO na kim .. 25 ..
